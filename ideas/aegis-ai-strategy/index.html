Aegis AI Governance & Readiness

Aegis is my governance and strategy workstream for organizations adopting AI in compliance-sensitive environments. It is not presented as a public code dump; the portfolio shows the product surface, assessment logic, and writing trail while keeping private implementation details separate.

Open Live App Readiness Survey Related Writing

Problem

AI adoption creates evidence debt

Teams can launch pilots faster than they can explain readiness, policy fit, risk ownership, human review, model behavior, and monitoring obligations. Aegis treats those artifacts as product requirements, not paperwork after the fact.

System

Strategy layer plus deployable controls

The work connects readiness scorecards, risk registers, pilot gates, ROI logic, audit trails, OCR/RAG review patterns, dashboards, and human-in-the-loop checkpoints into one operating model.

Audience

Leaders, reviewers, and builders

Aegis is designed for public-sector, healthcare, finance, and enterprise teams that need AI systems to be measurable, auditable, and explainable before they scale.

What It Demonstrates

Governance as an engineering surface

Assessment and readiness

Organization-level scoring, policy fit checks, implementation readiness, stakeholder risk, vendor exposure, and success metrics that make AI pilots comparable.

Risk and control design

Risk-register structure, human-review gates, data privacy controls, monitoring triggers, and audit evidence mapped to operational owners.

AI implementation patterns

OCR, RAG, document intelligence, multi-model evaluation, dashboards, and citation-backed review patterns that keep governance connected to working systems.

Executive translation

Framing that helps nontechnical leaders decide what to pilot, what to defer, what to monitor, and what evidence must exist before deployment.
