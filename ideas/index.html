The Lab

I actively explore venture concepts across consumer, AI, workflow, and culturally rooted markets, usually focusing on problems where behavior is changing faster than the products built around it. The work is less about copying visible startup trends and more about identifying where a new interface, intelligence layer, or system design could create meaningful commercial value.

Additional Venture Concepts

Ongoing

Beyond Aegis, the earlier Lab work explored how AI and workflow software could create value in public infrastructure planning, compliance systems, organizational memory, procurement visibility, and research-to-venture translation.

GovTech + Workflow Applied AI Systems

Market signal: Fragmented public-sector and knowledge-work software leaves room for vertical AI products that own workflow data, not only chat interfaces.

  • Public Infrastructure Copilot: Municipal planning software turning service requests and asset data into ranked capital priorities.

  • Operator Memory Cloud: Shared memory infrastructure for preserving decisions, context, and reusable execution logic.
  • Civic Procurement Graph: Procurement intelligence organizing bids, vendors, contracts, and agency activity into a searchable market map.
  • Research Commercialization Studio: Workflow support for turning academic research into venture-ready briefs and prototypes.
